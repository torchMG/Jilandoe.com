Bio

LANi is a West Coast rapper from Los Angeles crafting melodic, left-of-center records that blend rap, melody-driven hooks, and genre-bending production. A self-contained writer who pens all of his own music, he leans on conversational storytelling and memorable hooks, aiming for records that feel different while still landing with replay value and live-show energy.

Early records like “That’s It,” “Rules,” and “A Few Days” have started to build his foundation, earning support from DJ coalitions and a featured co-sign from Vic Mensa, who highlighted “Rules” on his fan spotlight segment. He has performed at Cali Fest and continues to grow his base through consistent performances at open mics, showcases, and local events, developing an early fan community known as The Roomies.

LANi is already making his presence felt—delivering records that work onstage and online, and turning first-time listeners into repeat supporters. With a defined creative lane, real traction from DJs and peers, and a fanbase that continues to deepen, he stands out as an emerging Los Angeles rapper built for the long run.
